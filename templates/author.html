Introduction

Famous authors have left lasting impacts on literature, shaping the world with their storytelling, unique viewpoints, and deep insights into the human experience. Here are some inspirational writers of all times who have written well-known books that we all treasure.

How to be like these authors

Read widely and deeply, and write regularly. Seek feedback from others and revise and edit. Explore new themes and ideas while staying true to yourself.

Writer's Block

You may come across writer's block, where you suddenly get a mind blank and stare at your entry unsure of what to write. To overcome this, a change of scenery or listening to music might help. Using prompts or establishing a writing routine can also stimulate ideas during these periods.
